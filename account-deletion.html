Delete Your Open Mining Account

You can permanently delete your Open Mining account directly from within the application.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Open Mining app.
  2. Go to the Profile screen.
  3. Tap the Delete Account option.
  4. Confirm your decision.
  5. Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted.
Important:

Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. Once deleted, your mining progress, rewards, referral data, and account information will no longer be recoverable.
